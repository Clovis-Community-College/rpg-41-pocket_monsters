The premise of the game is follow a group of heros who happen to be software engineers as well as students from the all famous Kerney. The world has entered into pure apoloypic chaos with rabies becoming even deadlier now, making the dogs super natural, some gain super human strenght and agility while others gain abilities like turning into a 1933 German or turning into beyblades. You and your party must rally together to survive this rabies infected world, to do so you must arrive on the safe heaven which is your professor's ranch.

Jacob worked on the front end of our code which is including but limited to the map, combat visualization.
Corbin made a circular linked list that handles who fights first in combat.
Yours truly, Logan, structured and realized our classes that contain the rabies infected dogs as well as our heros setting out to put them down to return the world back to normal. Additionally I also created an inventory BST that handles all the cool items our heros encounter out in their journey.

Our game is quiet simple you go around releasing the dogs from rabies. With the objective of finding a way to Kerney's ranch. The combat is turned based where each charcter chooses an attack move and target until all character on either side parish.

Suffering:

The classes all run off two parent classes called move and slop. Slop handles the key vraibles that all things have in our game such as hp and instinct. These two then go off to form the other two main classes which are our heroes called Software Engineers and Doggos. The move part of the classes handles everything that deals with the moves like dmg and if they are aoe or not. These two branches branch off into different directions software engineers create all the heroes, having their own unique class and the same applies to the doggo class which handles the creation of the different breeds of dogs you will encounter.
